Why is Task Management Essential?

Task management includes planning, analyzing, evaluating and reporting about a particular task’s progress. It an important aspect of the management a project because it helps to follow every task thoroughly. Regardless of the size and complexity of the task, task management is an absolute necessity when it comes to completing a project at time and budget.

In order for the process of task management to be effective and efficient, it is imperative that the project manager adheres to a schedule that will cover all important aspects of each task such as status, time, deadline, costs, and assigned resources.

Project management, process management, and task management are operations that are strongly interconnected between each other and can influence the project’s dynamics. Moreover, these processes often require a specific project management software so that project managers could regularly check the project’s activities and make proper reports.

Task management is useful and beneficial for different reasons such as prioritizing tasks, internal communication, project workflow, project participants’ performance, and more.

Some important points in task management:

  1. Prioritize tasks
  2. Better ‘project manager – team member’ communication
  3. Uninterrupted project flow
  4. High level of performance
  5. Fruitful team collaboration
  6. Resource allocation and time management

Final thoughts

Task management is a practical solution for both project managers and team members. It is proven that the working environment in which is implemented a project management software with task management feature is predetermined to reach success.

In fact, team members who are managing their tasks get real-time project status, continually evaluate their performance and build knowledge for future projects. Furthermore, project managers get the possibility to know the status and the % of completeness of each team member project’s tasks and give feedback and comments for the accomplished work.
